About the School

The School of Computer Science & Engineering at IILM University, Gurugram offers cutting-edge academic programs designed to prepare students for the future of technology. Our B Tech in Computer Science & Engineering includes specializations in:

- Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML)
- Data Science & Big Data Analytics
- Cloud Computing
- CyberSecurity
- Robotics
- Data Engineering
- Full-Stack Development
- Generative AI

We also offer M Tech and PhD programs for advanced research and academic pursuits. Under the visionary leadership of Dr. Shamik Tiwari, Founding Dean, the school emphasizes innovation, research, and industry collaboration. Strategic partnerships with leading companies such as HCL, Microsoft, Xebia, and L&T ensure that students gain real-world exposure and hands-on experience. The school hosts three Centers of Excellence (CoEs):

- Center of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence (AI): Focuses on machine learning, deep learning, data analytics, natural language processing, and intelligent systems.
- Center of Excellence in CyberSecurity and Digital Forensics: Covers cybersecurity, ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, cyber laws, and digital forensics.
- Center of Excellence in Robotics and Automation: Supports learning and research in robotics, embedded systems, industrial automation, control systems, IoT, and Robot Operating System (ROS).
